Project relate questions

Tell us about your favorite project experience - something that was exciting, energizing, and personally satisfying.

My favorite project was the latest one, developing a react and spring-based webcam personal fitness training website. I had a great team to work on: (1) building an interactive, scalable website to support user registration, training schedule and video stream with a trainer based on React, Redux, and Spring; (2) implementing a schedule calendar with big-react0calendar; (3) using OpenTok API to implement classroom video chat function. This project expanded my knowledge and enhanced my programming capabilities during above-mentioned steps. The most exciting and energizing parts were that our team did a great job of building a project from scratch and I was trained by a professional software engineer during this process. Through the whole progress, our working efficiency was emphasized by using (1) Sneakpeekit to sketch the wireframe of our website; (2) Google Docs to update daily progress and difficulties; (3) Zoom to set up engineer meeting on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday; (4) Git to handle version-control, track changes of each file; (5) PowerPoint to present to the project manager at the end. I believe I will put my best into this internship and diverse skills into practice as a prepared software engineer for your company.

Please give an example of something you changed/improved for the better.

Completing a project with limited teammates was challenging, our team started with eight people but ended with four. For their personal reasons, four members left before hitting the mid-range of the project. Initially, due to the limited time period and technical difficulties, these issues led to our lack of motivation in continuously achieving the project. As a software engineer, I suffered from not being productive for our team, not standing up for our team, and not being helpful for the other three teammates who also lost confidence. It was not until then that I realized the significance of leadership and decided to fight for this situation. I decided to have a conversation with our project manager to come up with a new minimum viable product. In order to deploy the project on time, picking up on what they remained was necessary. Therefore, I did more jobs on video stream API, testing existed code and communicating between front-end and back-end people. Thanks to my last three teammates, we were able to implement this interactive, scalable website project successfully in an extremely short time.
